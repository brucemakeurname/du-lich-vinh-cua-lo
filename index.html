Rui ro

Danh Gia Rui Ro

Nhung dieu can chu y de chuyen di an toan va vui ve

✅ Rui Ro THAP

  • Thang 4 ngoai mua mua bao (mua bao: T8-T11)
  • Song nhe, nuoc bien am - phu hop tam bien
  • Cua Lo co 16 tram tam nuoc ngot mien phi
  • Co luc luong cuu ho tren bai bien

⚡ Rui Ro TRUNG BINH

  • Dong duc: Le 30/4-1/5 la dip nghi le lon nhat
  • Gia tang: Phong, ve xe, do an tang 20-50%
  • Giao thong: Duong HN-Vinh co the ket xe, +1-2 tieng
  • Nang nong: UV cao, can kem chong nang SPF50+
  • Co the co mua rao ngan vao buoi chieu

🔶 CAN DE PHONG

  • Chi tam khu vuc co co xanh, co cuu ho. KHONG tam dem
  • Doi mu bao hiem, KHONG uong bia roi lai xe
  • Can than do dac ca nhan tren bai bien dip le dong
  • Mang thuoc chong say xe, thuoc tieu hoa
  • Backup tien mat (nhieu quan nho khong nhan CK)
